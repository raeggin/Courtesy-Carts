Courtesy Cart Mission


We crafted a custom grocery list organizer that allows users to create grocery list. The user has the ability to add items , enter the quantity of each item to be purchased, mark items as purchased and remove items from their shopping list. Our target audience for this service consist of the high- risk COVID-19 population , as well as , those who do not have access to a grocery store or time to allocate to grocery shopping. Nevertheless , virtually anyone old enough to grocery shop with a cellular device or computer can use Courtesy Carts application.

How It Works


Step 1:Create an account / Sign In.

Step2: Click Tab [ located in top right corner of webpage in navigation menu ]

Step 3: Select the logo for the store you would like to order from and click when you are ready to make your grocery list.

Step 4: Type in item names and quantity of each item you would like to purchase.

Step 5: Delete any items from the cart using trash icon.

Step 6: Use checkboxes to manually mark items as purchased. Click when you are done making your grocery list and ready to save.
